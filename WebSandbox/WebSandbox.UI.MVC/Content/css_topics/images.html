Images

Implementing images on a site makes the content more engaging and can sometimes help deliver ideas in a more concise manner. There are 3 primary image types that are widely used in development.

JPG or JPEG

  • Highly compressed
  • The standard for many image formats because they are a good comprimise between quality and size.
  • Lossy compression: making changes and resaving the file results in a degredation of image quality.
  • Do not support animation.
  • Do not support transparency.

GIF

  • Lossless compression
  • Small file size, but poor image quality.
  • They support transparency.
  • They support animation.

PNG

  • Very high quality and have a large file size.
  • Lossless compression
  • Do not support animation.
  • They do support transparency.
